## Our Room Rates

- Are in USD ($).
- Include breakfast (for two), WiFi, service charge and taxes.
- No charge for children under 2 years unless a bed is required.
- Bunk beds or fold-out beds are provided as follows:
  1. Up to the number of adults/children shown in the room occupancy.
  2. For children between 2 and 12 years, the rate is $43.50 per night.
  3. For guests 13 years and above, the rate is $73.90 per night.
  4. The above rates include breakfast, tax and service charge.

  Includes: breakfast, light afternoon tea and dinner.

## Additional Information

- Check-in: after 3pm.
- Check-out: by 11am.
- Early check-in / late check-out will be offered, pending availability, at 50% of the contracted nightly rate.
- Driver's accommodation is not available on the property but can be arranged close by in either Koslanda or Haputale.
- Rates are subject to change without notice.

## Cancellation Policy

**Two rooms or fewer**

- Cancellation more than 31 days before arrival incurs a $20.00 admin fee.
- Cancellation 30 days or fewer before arrival incurs a charge of 100%.

**Peak season and/or 3 or more rooms**

- Cancellation more than 45 days before arrival incurs a $20.00 admin fee.
- Cancellation 31 to 45 days before arrival incurs a charge of 25%.
- Cancellation 30 days or fewer before arrival incurs a charge of 100%.

## Payment Policy

- Full payment is required 30 days before arrival.
- During peak periods, full payment is required 60 days before arrival.
- Failure to make payment may invalidate the booking.
- Bookings are only confirmed on receipt by the booking party of an official The Planters House invoice. Sending of a booking voucher is not considered confirmation of a booking.
